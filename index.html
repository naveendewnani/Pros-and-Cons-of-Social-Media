Pros and Cons of

Social Media

Read below ↓

Pros

● Social media spreads information faster than any other media.
● Social media allows people make new friends.
● Social media sites helps businesses to expand.
● Social media facilitates face-to-face interaction.
● Social media helps to create awareness among the pepople.
● Social media allows for rapid communication during crisis events.
● Social media helps people who are socially isolated or shy connect with other people.

Cons

● Social media can contribute to social isolation
● Social media Presents a false idea of “friendship”.
● Social media can increase feelings of depression and anxiety.
● Social media can spread misinformation to large amounts of people.
● Social media can lead to addiction
● Social media users are vulnerable to security attacks such as hacking.
● Social media Conveys Inauthentic Expression of Feelings
